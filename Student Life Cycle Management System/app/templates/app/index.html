Student Life Cycle Management System

Student Life Cycle Management System or SLCM is a website desgined to cater to the academic needs of a student studying at a university.

Not sure how well you performed in your last test?

SLCM provides a powerful user interface which allows students to view how well they've performed and where they stand after each test.

Feeling a little under the weather or planning a trip with your friends?

Check your attendance and see how many classes you have attended and whether or not you can bunk your next lecture!
